Believe it or not, you're currently looking at this project right now! This project is the website you're currently reading! During my first year at OSU, we had to write a little bit of HTML as part of some projects, and I discovered how simple it was! I decided to create this website as a place for me to keep a blog, show off my projects, and to have a signle place where I can point people to who want to know more about me, or see more of what I do.
